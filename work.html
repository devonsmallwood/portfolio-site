Games

Possessor

Possessor is a short virtual reality puzzle platformer game developed with Unity XR. It was designed for people like myself who experience motion sickness from free movement in mind. The game's possession system allows the player to solve physics-based platforming puzzles without movement of the camera, which is generally what causes motion sickness.
