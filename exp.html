devon smallwood

designer | developer | musical artist

experience

While at Boise State University, Devon focused primarily on game and web development, with an emphasis on Unity, C#, HTML, and CSS. He is proficient with the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite, Microsoft Office, and G Suite.

Devon has made several small games using Unity, Unreal Engine 4, and Adobe Animate. He used programs such as Audacity and Adobe Audition, as well as the real-time sound synthesis programming language ChucK, to create sound effects and music for his games.

He has experience developing websites with Wordpress, as well as designing websites both from scratch and using Bootstrap templates.

Devon also has worked with teams to design apps for both iOS and Android.

He is familiar with XCode and Swift and, utilizing Apple's ARKit, worked with other students to create a prototype for an augmented reality iOS app to help children with learning disabilities learn how to spell.

He also worked with a team to develop a basic Android app to keep record of lost dogs with Ionic and Firebase, using Google Maps API.
